BEFORE YOU SHARE

A few honest answers

What exactly do I send?

A photo and a few sentences — a memory, a goodbye letter, whatever feels right. There's no length requirement and nothing has to be polished.

Where does it get posted?

On our Memory Wall on Instagram and Facebook, so their story can be seen — and so others who feel the same ache can find it.

Is it still okay if it's been years?

Yes. Grief doesn't run on a schedule. Share whenever you're ready, even decades later.

Can I have it taken down later?

Yes. Just email us and we'll remove it, no questions asked.
