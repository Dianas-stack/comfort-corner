BEFORE YOU JOIN

A few honest answers

Is this therapy or a support group?

No. It's a community of people who understand what you're carrying — not a clinical service. If you need professional support, we'll always point you toward it.

Will I get spammed with sales emails?

No. This list exists for connection, not for selling to you. Just honest notes and a place to land when you need one.

What if I'm not ready to share yet?

You don't have to. Read, sit with it, and join the conversation whenever — or if — you're ready. There's no pressure here.

Can I leave anytime?

Yes. One click, no questions asked, no guilt trip on the way out.
