❦ How it works

Comfort, in three quiet steps

No fuss, no pressure. Just something steady you can hold onto.

Step one

Find your words

Browse comforts made for a specific kind of loss, a mom, a dad, a child, a partner, a pet, a dear friend. Pick the one that feels like yours.

Step two

Make it yours

Add a name, a date, or a pet's photo wherever you can. Many pieces can be personalized, so it speaks to your person, not just anyone.

Step three

Keep it close

However it comes to you, keep it somewhere you'll see it. Tuck it by a photo, in a journal, on a wall, or beside the bed.
